African Elephants Learning Quiz

Take this quiz to learn more about African Elephants!

An average African Elephant weighs 6 tons. That about three times as heavy as a giraffe!

The average adult African elephant eats about 300 pounds of plant material a day. Even though they eat so much, their digestive efficiency is very low.

An African elephant carried a calf in the womb for 22 months. When the calf is born, its average weight is 200 pounds.

The oldest females lead the elephant family groups. This is because the elephant development is similar to human development. Elephants gain more wisdom with age, so the oldest, and therefore wisest, elephants lead the groups.

An African elephant poops 12 to 15 times a day. That's about 220 to 250 pounds of manure. In a year, a grown African Elephant produces more than 40 tons of manure!
